About Symposium

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Anna University Regional Campus Madurai is conducting a National Level Technical Symposium through the Association of "ICONICS". In this Symposium, we are conducting both technical and non-technical events. Students from engineering, Arts and Science colleges are invited and encouraged to participate in this events. Registration fees Rs.100/- per head and Rs.150/- per team(2 members). Payment should be done digitally using QR code prior to the event date. Fill out the registration form and upload the payment screenshot in the google form. Read the description and rules of the event. Keep in contact with us on social media.

Technical Events

Bug Bounty

Participants should debug the given programs within the time limit given. Winners of the event will be decided according to the correctness and optimization of the code. Two winners will be selected from the event.

Rules

  • Participants should submit the answer sheets within time limit.
  • Any queries in the event should be immediately made known to the volunteers.
  • Judges decision is final.

Paper enzo

Participants can select a topic from the given topics. The PPT's can be Presented both in online and offline mode. Two winners will be selected from the uniqueness on their PPT shown.

Rules

  • Minimum number of slides should be 12.
  • The maximum time for a participants is 10 minutes.
  • PPT's should be sent to the co-ordinators 30 Minutes prior to the event time.
  • Judges decision is final.
TOPICS
Block Chain Data Science Machine learning
Internet Of Things Cloud computing AR/VR
Artificial Intelligence Networking Phishing
Cyber Security

Typing Prowess

Participants are given with passage or paragraph and they should type it in the given system within the time allotted. The winners are selected according to the correctness of text and proficiency of typing.

Rules

  • The numbers of round will be three.
  • Time given for first round is 3 minutes.
  • Decision of Judges is final.

Webcraft

The Participants are given with a sample front end design. Participants should recreate the design within the given time limit. Winners will be selected based on the correctness of the given design. From among the contestants, two winners will be chosen.

Rules

  • Time given for the event is 45 minutes.
  • VScode ,Sublime can be used.
  • Winners will be selected by panel of judges
  • Decision of Judges is final.

Non Technical Events

Color Artist

Participants should paint the given theme without brush or any painting tools. The Participants should complete their art within the given time. Two winners will be selected from the participants.

Rules

  • Time given for event will be 15 minutes.
  • Winners will be selected by panel of judges.
  • Judges decision is final.

Connections

Participants are shown with 3 or more than 3 connecting pictures which together gives names, songs or word which is the answer. The participants with highest points will be declared as winners. Two winners will be selected from the participants.

Rules

  • Participants who answer first will be given points.
  • Total number of rounds will be Three.
  • Judges decision is final.

Brain Strike

The Participants are shown with a picture consisting of many items. Participants are made to write the items shown on the picture in sheet. whoever writes the highest answers will be selected for next round. Two winners will be selected from the participants.

Rules

  • Total Number of rounds will be three.
  • Time given for First round will be one minute.
  • Time given for second round will be Three minutes.
  • Time given for third round will be Five minutes.
  • Judges decision is final.

Decode the Case

The Participants are given with a solved mystery(case) from the past students should solve the case and decode the answers from it. Participant whoever gives answer correct or close to the original answer will be declared as winner. Two winners will be selected from the participants

Rules

  • Total Number of Rounds will be Three.
  • Time given for first round will be 5 minutes.
  • The given for third round will be 10 minutes.
  • Judges decision is final.
